About Me

A passionate individual at the intersection of technology, education, and creative expression.

I am currently pursuing my Master's in Smart Manufacturing at the prestigious Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. My academic journey began with a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from BIT Sindri, where I built a strong foundation in technical principles.

My passion extends beyond my core field into the realms of education and content creation. I believe in the power of sharing knowledge, which led me to work as a Physics Educator at Unacademy and to create platforms like NEET VERSE. Currently, I document my life and learnings at IISc through my channel, IISc Sparks ✨.

As an author, I've explored my creative side by writing "THE ENIGMA." I am driven by a desire to innovate, inspire, and contribute meaningfully, whether it's through academic research, teaching, or digital content.
